Once you have uploaded all the required documents, I will be going through your negative items on your report one by one and disputing any incorrect information, collections, late payments and any negative public records on your behalf.

You will get an email notification once I have completed this work and have sent it off to all the credit bureaus.

Here is what you can expect over the next 30-45 days:

Responses from the Credit Bureaus, Creditors & Collection Agengies

You will start receiving mail after we submit Round 1 that will show if any items we disputed have been removed or updated.
   

Possible credit score improvement!

By following the credit re-establishment plan, you can positively impact your credit scores quickly. 

Results from the credit dispute process don't occur until after the credit bureau's required investigation timeframe of 30 days. 

Things you SHOULD do...
Try to pay down your credit card balances - the ideal percentage of use is between 1%-9%, so if you have a card with a $1000 limit - try to get the balance down to around $90 if possible.
Continue to make all your credit payments on-time. If you encounter a financial hardship, contact your creditors immediately for assistance options.

Things you should NOT do...
Apply for any new credit cards, loans or lines of credit that are NOT outlined in your Credit Re-Establishment Plan- this can have a negative impact on your current scores.
   

 

Do not close any of your credit accounts - this can have a negative impact as well.

Contact your Credit Repair Specialist with any questions regarding what accounts you should or shouldn't pay.

IMPORTANT! 

If you receive any mail responses, please take a photo, or scan them into your computer and upload them to your Client Portal under the "Documents" section.
